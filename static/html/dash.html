👤 Welcome !

Welcome to your dashboard! As a commission member, you'll participate in secure secret sharing. Here's your workflow:

  1. Accept Invitations: Join commissions when invited by admin
  2. Wait for Marche: Admin will initiate a marche event when ready
  3. Process Shares: Once marche starts, you'll receive shares to process
  4. Participate: Help reconstruct secrets when enough members are ready

All operations use elliptic curve cryptography (P-256) for security. Your private keys never leave your device.

As a commission member, you'll help create a secure key pair for encrypting sensitive public data. You'll receive two invitation types:

  • 1 For the "marche" to create the public key
  • 2 For reconstruction of the private key

Marche Accepted Successfully

You will now begin the process of creating the shares and broadcasting them. Once completed, the admin will publish the public key.

You'll receive notifications when further actions are required, and can track progress in the system.

Reconstruction Accepted Successfully

Your share will be used to reconstruct the secret key. Once the threshold of accepted shares is reached:

  • • The admin will display the secret key
  • • You'll be notified when complete

Rejection Successful
