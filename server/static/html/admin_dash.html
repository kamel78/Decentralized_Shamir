🛠️ Admin Dashboard – Welcome & Functionalities

Welcome, Admin! You are the coordinator of secure collaboration within the platform. Your first responsibility is to create commissions—groups of users who will later help reconstruct cryptographic secrets. When creating a commission, you define key parameters such as the number of members (n) and the threshold (t) required to reconstruct the secret. Once the commission is set up, you invite users to join. Once they accept, they become active members of the group.

After forming a commission, you initiate the "marche" phase, during which members are notified and gain the ability to generate and share their cryptographic secrets. You control this transition and ensure proper participation. As users process incoming shares and update their state, the system monitors progress. Once the required number of members (t) have completed their part, the shared secret can be reconstructed. All data exchanges are encrypted using elliptic curve cryptography (P-256), ensuring full decentralization and zero single point of failure. From your dashboard, you can manage commissions, launch marches, track participation, and oversee the overall security protocol.
