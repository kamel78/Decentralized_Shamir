Important: About Your Password

Our system uses opaque authentication — your password is never stored directly. Instead:

  • A cryptographic export key is derived from your password
  • This key is essential for accessing your security shares and encrypted transactions
  • If you forget your password and reset it, a new export key will be generated
  • This means you will lose access to your previous shares and transactions

Note: While you can reset your password, it will not restore your original export key or previous data.

Please make sure to:

  • Remember your password securely
  • Consider saving it in a password manager
  • Keep a backup in a secure location
